Frequently Asked Questions

Our production time varies based on the complexity of your photo book:

  • Standard Orders: 3-5 business days for production + shipping time
  • Express Orders: 1-2 business days for production + shipping time
  • Rush Orders: Same day production + overnight shipping

Shipping times depend on your location and chosen shipping method.

We support most common image formats:

  • Formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, TIFF, BMP
  • Recommended Resolution: 300 DPI for best quality
  • Minimum Size: 800x600 pixels
  • Maximum File Size: 10MB per image

For optimal results, we recommend using high-resolution images (300 DPI or higher).

Yes, you can edit your photo book up until it goes into production:

  • Before Production: Full editing capabilities
  • During Production: Limited changes (contact support)
  • After Production: No changes possible

You'll receive an email notification when your order goes into production.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee:

  • Quality Issues: Free replacement or full refund
  • Customer Satisfaction: 30-day money-back guarantee
  • Return Shipping: Free for quality issues
  • Processing Time: 3-5 business days for refunds

Contact our support team to initiate a return or refund.

You can track your order in several ways:

  • Dashboard: View all orders in your account dashboard
  • Email Updates: Automatic notifications at each stage
  • Tracking Number: Use the provided tracking number with the carrier
  • Support Team: Contact us for assistance

You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Bank Transfers: Available for large orders
  • Gift Cards: PhotoBook gift cards accepted

All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
